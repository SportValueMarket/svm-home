OUR STORY

the idea

We are from different backgrounds but we have witnessed the emergence of blockchain and online sport betting. So we found two interesting opportunities:

1/ fixed odds bets

In sports betting, bookmakers offer fixed odds bets: you buy the right to win an amount based on a game result. In betting exchanges you can also short sell (lay) and offer your own odds (or price). But there is no real difference between different platforms in terms of product.

2/ digital assets

The other big trend is the emergence of blockchain technology and the ability to create digital assets as tokens on a public blockchain. So we came up with the idea of tokenising sport players, but not as unique tokens like in fantasy football, but as entirely new tradable asset class.

TOKENISING SPORTS

So we came up with the idea of tokenising sport players, but not as unique tokens like in fantasy football, but as entirely new tradable asset class. Each token is a virtual asset that entitles its holder to financial payouts based entirely on the personal performance of the player for each future game.
